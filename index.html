Win a Limited Edition T-shirt by contributing

Steps to be followed



  1. Log into Hacktoberfest website using your Github profile
  2. Contribute to a minimun of 4 repositories under 'Hacktoberfest' by forking a repository of your choice, cloning, and making a pull request.
    Read updated rules
  3. Pull requests considered spam will not be accepted.
  4. Check the status of your pull requests under your profile in the official Hacktoberfest website
  5. Once atleast 4 of your pull requests are verified, you will be eligible either to get a free t-shirt or plant a tree in your name instead! Checkout FAQs here
  6. Finding it difficult to contribute as a beginner? You can look for beginner-friendly issues here

Plant a tree in the Hacktober Forest



"When we plant trees, we plant the seeds of peace and hope"

-Wangari Maathai

Trees and forests are the home for many birds and animals, and cutting them down destroys their habitat. By planting more trees and building new forests we can save many species that are dependent on such lifestyles that include tree as a home and their fruits, leaves, etc., as their food. This will maintain the biodiversity in our world and the harmony of life.

CHOOSE TREES OR TEES: Rather than receive t-shirts as swag, you can choose to have a tree planted in your name and help make Hacktoberfest 2021 more carbon neutral.

So, choose a tree reward for this Hacktoberfest and become an Eco-Warrior.
